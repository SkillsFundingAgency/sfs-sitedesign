How recoupment for this opening period is calculated

Recoupment is calculated using the following equation:

(Post de-delegation budget − Rates Liability) = Full year recoupment

We then pro-rata this for the days the academy has been open:

(Full year recoupment ÷ 365) × Days open = Recoupment adjusted pro rata

We finally add the de-delegated funding pro-rata for 7 months of the year:

(Recoupment adjusted pro rata+7 months de-delegation) +/− Net anomalies = Total recoupment
