How this recoupment is calculated

We will recoup the de-delegated funding for the period September 2021 to March 2022 using the following equations:

(Post de-delegation budget − Rates) + (Rates × 0.2) = Full year recoupment

We then pro-rata this into days that the academy is open.

(Full year recoupment ÷ 365) × Days open = Recoupment adjusted pro rata

We finally add the de-delegated funding pro-rata.

7/12 of de-delegation+ Recoupment adjusted pro rata = Total recoupment
