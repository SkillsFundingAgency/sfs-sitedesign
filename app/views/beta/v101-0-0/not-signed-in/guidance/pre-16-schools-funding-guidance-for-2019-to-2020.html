Details

Operational guidance for 2019 to 2020

Local authorities and their schools forums should use this guide to plan the local implementation of the funding system for the 2019 to 2020 financial year.

Disapplication proforma

Local authorities should use this form to make a formal request to the Secretary of State to disapply any part of The School and Early Years Finance (England) Regulations.

Further information

Read the ministerial statement for information on the schools revenue funding arrangements for 2019 to 2020.

The operational guide for the 2018 to 2019 financial year is available.
