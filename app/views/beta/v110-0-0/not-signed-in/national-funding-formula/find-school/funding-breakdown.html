How these figures are calculated

The funding the school actually receives may be different

These figures show what schools would receive if the national funding formula was followed exactly and 2019 pupil numbers were used. The funding that schools actually receive may be different. This is because funds are allocated to the local authority first. The local authority then decides how much funding a school gets.

Pupil numbers are two years old

These figures use calculations based on 2019 pupil numbers. The school’s final funding allocation for 2021 to 2022 will be based on pupil numbers from 2020.

Why are pupil numbers calculated this way?

At the time the national funding formula information is prepared, the most recent pupil number information available is from the previous year's October census or agreed pupil numbers from a similar time. By the time the final funding statement is issued, more recent pupil numbers are available to prepare the final calculations.

What these allocation figures include

  • funding for 5 to 16 year olds
  • the teachers’ pay grant
  • the teachers’ pension employer contribution grant 

Figures for 2020 to 2021 include the teachers’ pay grant and the teachers’ pension employer contribution grant. This ensures that the figures for 2020 to 2021 and 2021 to 2022 are comparable, since the funding from these grants has been added to the national funding formula allocations for 2021 to 2022.

Allocation figures don’t include

  • funding for pre-school or post-16
  • pupil premium
  • PE and sport premium
  • high needs
  • special educational needs and disabilities (SEND) funding
  • funding for other types of schools, such as special schools and pupil referral units

If a school isn’t open all year

For schools that opened or will permanently close partway through the year, the funding allocation shown is the funding allocated for the school for the period it is open. The funding per pupil figure shows the amount that the school would have received for each pupil had it been open for the full year.
