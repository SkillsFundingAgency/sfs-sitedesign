National funding formula allocations 2021 to 2022

Calculations show what schools would receive if the national funding formula was followed exactly and 2019 pupil numbers were used. The funding that schools receive may be different. This is because funds are allocated to the local authority first. The local authority then decides how much funding a school gets.

These calculations are based on 2019 pupil numbers. The final funding allocations will be based on pupil numbers from 2020.
