View national funding formula allocations for your school: 2021 to 2022

Find out how much funding is allocated for your school using the national funding formula.

The schools national funding formula is the way the government decides how much core funding to allocate for mainstream, state-funded schools in England.

You can view funding for:

  • schools maintained by a local authority
  • academies
  • free schools

The funding covers 5 to 16 year olds.
