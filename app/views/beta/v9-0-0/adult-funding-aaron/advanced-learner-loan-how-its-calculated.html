How we have calculated your advanced learner loan facility

We have set your baseline using your approved, attended and paid loan applications in 2016 to 2017. If you meet our track-record criteria we have increased your baseline by 3%, otherwise you receive the baseline.

We have then applied one of the following rules:

  • if we awarded you growth at performance management point 1 or 2 of the 2017 to 2018 academic year, or your contract value did not change at these points, your facility will be the greater of your baseline or your post-performance management point 2 contract value. If we awarded you growth at performance management point 1 but then reduced your facility at performance management point 2, we will still apply this rule.
  • if we reduced your contract value at performance management point 2 of the 2017 to 2018 academic year, your facility will be the lower of your baseline or your post performance management point 2 contract value.
