Education and skills funding

The ESFA brings together the Education Funding Agency and Skills Funding Agency to create an agency responsible for funding education and skills for children, young people and adults.


PE and sport premium 2018 to 2019

The PE and sport premium allocations 2018 to 2019 shows how much funding the following types of school will get:

  • schools maintained by the local authority
  • academies and free schools
  • non-maintained special schools for children with special educational needs
  • city technology colleges
  • pupil referral units (PRUs provide education for children who can’t go to a mainstream school)
  • general hospitals
